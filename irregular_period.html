Irregular Period


It is ok if your period is slightly off since menstrual cycle is not always predictable. There are many factors that can play into why your period is irregular such as hormone levels, stress, certain health conditions, and medications. If you notice that your period is really irredular then it is a good idea to consult with a health professional.
