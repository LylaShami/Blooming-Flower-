Period in Your 40s


In your 40s is when you will start to see changes in your period. This including perimenopausal hormonal fluctuations which is a precursor signs of menopause. In your late 50s is when menopause usually hits. You will eventually noy have your period since your ovaries will stop producing eggs. For more information consult with a health professor.
