Symptom: Insomnia


Insomnia during periods, also known as period insomnia, is when you have sleep issues that that might be attributed to PMS (premenstrual syndrome) or RMDD (premenstrual dysphoric disorder). To learn more about both PMS and PMDD you can talk to a health professional or refer to the information in the soruce below. If you have Insomnia, it is a good idea to speak to your doctor. Insomnia can occur not only during your period but also before you period. Some ways known to help with help you combat insomnia includes avoiding daytime naps, have a set schedule for waking up and sleeping everyday, avoid tv, mobile phones, or even reading in bed during bedtime, avoid drinking caffein later in the day and more. You can learn more about insomnia and how it is related to periods in the source provided below. You can also talk to a health professional about insomnia as well.
