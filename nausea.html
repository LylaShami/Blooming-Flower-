Symptom: Nausea


Nausea during your period can be caused by many different things. One of the most common cause of nausea during your period is dysmenorrhea which is painful menstrual cramps. dysmenorrhea is caused by the increase of uterus contractions. This happens when there are a large of amount of prostaglandins. Menstrual cramps can be felt in the lower abdomen, hips, thighs, or back.
