Our Mission

Our mission is to provide accurate and accessible information about periods, including menstrual health, hygiene, and management, in order to promote menstrual equity and empower individuals to take control of their menstrual health.

Our mission is to break the stigma surrounding periods by providing education and resources that promote understanding and acceptance of menstruation as a natural and normal bodily function.

Our mission is to support individuals who menstruate by providing comprehensive and evidence-based information about periods, as well as resources for managing menstrual symptoms and accessing menstrual products.

Our mission is to create a community of individuals who are passionate about menstrual health and equity, by providing a platform for sharing experiences, knowledge, and resources related to periods.
