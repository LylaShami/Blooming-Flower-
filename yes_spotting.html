Spotting Outside of Periods


There are many reasons why you might have spottings between you periods. Things like the changes in your hormone levels, the use hormonal contraception devices, an infection, or an injury. However there many other reasons, including the inflammation of your cervix, abnormalities in the cervix or uterus or even an miscarriage.
