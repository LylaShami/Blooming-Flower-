Symptom: Bloating


Bloating is a common symptom to have during your menstrual cycle. Sometimes it may feel like weight gain as your abdominal or other parts of your body feels tight or even swollen. Usually bloating occurs only when your periods starts and will eventually go away. There is no real way of avoiding bloating but there are some ways to reduce bloating. Some of them includes drinking a lot of water, skip caffeine or alcohol, exercise, and having a low sodium diet.
