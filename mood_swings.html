Symptom: Mood Swings


Mood swings are one of the many behavioral syptoms a woman may have during their periods. Some other behavioral syptoms include depression, anger, anxiety, feeling overwhelmed or even having difficulty concentrating. Some of the causes to these behavioral symptoms are the changes in hormones and chemical changes to the brain. If these behavioral symptoms are affecting your health or your daily activities in any way, please consult with a health professional about your symptoms.
