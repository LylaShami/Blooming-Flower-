Symptom: Fatigue


Fatigue is one of the most common symptomes of PMS (premenstrual syptoms). Usually fatigues happens before your period. Fatigue before periods usually happens because of the lack of serotonin in your brain. Before your period every month the level of serotonin in your brain fluctuates and therefore affecting your mood. This can lead to energy dips which in turn causes fatigue. ANother reason why you may have fatigue during your period is because of sleep issues during your period. Sleep issues can be caused by other premenstrual symptoms such as cramps, bloating, and headaches. Your body temperature also increases during your period whihc therefore can effect your sleep also. Some tips to fighting fatigue is to create a strict bedtime routine, exercise, reduce sugar in your diet, stay hydrated and more.
